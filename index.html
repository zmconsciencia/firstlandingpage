I'm A Pyke God
Abandoned by his crew to the leviathans of the deep, Pyke drowned—but didn’t stay dead. Years later, a fabled revenant known as the Bloodharbor Ripper still stalks the Slaughter Docks, crossing names off a list that never seems to end. Wait...you look familiar...
Pyke Abilities
BONE SKEWER
Pyke either stabs an enemy in front of him or pulls an enemy towards him.
GHOSTWATER DIVE
Pyke enters Camouflage and gains significant movement speed that decays over time.
PHANTOM UNDERTOW
Pyke dashes and leaves behind a phantom that will return to him, stunning enemy champions along its path.
DEATH FROM BELOW
Pyke blinks to and executes low health enemies, allowing him to cast this spell again and granting additional gold to an ally who assists.
Renowned harpooner from the slaughter docks of Bilgewater, Pyke should have met his death in the belly of a gigantic jaull-fish… and yet, he returned. Now, stalking the dank alleys and backways of his former hometown, he uses his new supernatural gifts to bring a swift and gruesome end to those who make their fortune by exploiting others—and a city that prides itself on hunting monsters now finds a monster hunting them.
